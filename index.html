How It Works

From observation to report
in seconds.

1

Select Your Inspection Module

Choose from Galley, Bars, Restaurants, Potable Water, RWF, Medical, Housekeeping, and more. Every module is pre-loaded with VSP 2025 checklist items.

2

Record or Type Your Observation

Speak naturally — "Three compartment sink chlorine bucket at 0 ppm in crew galley" — or type it. Pulse automatically transcribes and normalizes your input.

3

AI Instantly Classifies the Finding

Pulse matches your observation to the correct VSP 2025 reference, assigns a risk level, and writes deficiency wording — all powered by GPT-4o.

4

Generate & Export Your Report

Review all findings, edit as needed, then export a professional Excel report or print directly. Everything is structured for VSP submission.
